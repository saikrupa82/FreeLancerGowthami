The Origin of vivekananda

Vivekananda Degree College was established in 2005 to promote quality, modem and technical education attend to the needs of changing times under the esteemed management of the Noble Group of Modern institutions. The college strives to provide opportunities to students to realize their potential in curricular and extra curricular activities and actualize their aspirations in life. Our college is named after a great ascetic Swamy Vivekananda, who rocked entire world with his austerity and ever inspiring sayings which still echo in the deep comers of the hearts of the people
